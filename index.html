Welcome to the Car Selection Application!

This application demonstrates PHP session management by allowing you to:

  • Select multiple cars from a list of 7 automobiles
  • Store your selections in a PHP session array
  • View your selected cars on a separate display page
  • Clear your selections when needed
  • Navigate between pages while maintaining your session data

Application Features:

Car Selection Page: Choose from 7 different automobiles using a multiselect interface
Session Storage: Selected cars are stored in $_SESSION['selected_cars'] array
Car Display Page: View all your selected cars with clear formatting
Clear Function: Option to clear all current car selections
Navigation: Easy links to move between selection and display pages
